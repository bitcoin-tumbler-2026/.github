## What Is a Bitcoin Tumbler?

A **Bitcoin tumbler** is a privacy service designed to break the direct link between your sending and receiving Bitcoin addresses. It achieves this by mixing your coins with those of many other users in a shared pool. Once the process is complete, you receive clean Bitcoin that cannot be easily traced back to the original source.

This technique provides plausible deniability and makes blockchain analysis much more difficult.

## Bitcoin Mixer vs Bitcoin Tumbler – What’s the Difference?

In reality, there is very little difference between the two terms. “Bitcoin tumbler” is simply an older, more traditional name for the same type of service. Both mixers and tumblers serve the same purpose: to obfuscate transaction history and protect user privacy.

Today’s advanced **Bitcoin tumblers** use improved technology, including larger liquidity pools and better privacy protocols, compared to services from previous years.
